Javier Piqueras is a Senior Software Engineer with 10+ years of professional experience. He is building a personal portfolio website to attract potential clients and employers in the tech industry.

He works incrementally and deliberately — specifications are provided step by step. He does not want more steps taken than instructed.

Design taste leans toward high-quality, modern developer aesthetics (referenced Vercel Ship typography style as inspiration). He is comfortable with technical implementation details and appreciates direct, actionable feedback.

The portfolio is single-page (all sections accessible via scroll or anchor), multilingual (English default, Spanish toggle), and deployed on Vercel.
